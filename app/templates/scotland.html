Scotland

September 2020

I have spent the vast majority of my 30+ years on this planet avoiding Scotland. I attribute this to a scarring childhood holiday spent in a tent in the rain. However, with the travel restrictions imposed by the COVID pandemic, I figured it was worth giving Scotland a second chance. I spent 8 days hiking in Glencoe, and on the islands of Kerrera and Seil. The weather was often wet, but the scenery was stunning.

The following photos were shot on a Nikon D600 with a Nikon 16-35mm f/4 and Nikon 80-200mm f/2.8.
